Social Media Aggregator

A way to gain a clear view on your business environment in the social media landscape without having to do a painstaking amount of google search work.

The project

DataScouts Social Media Aggregator allows you to aggregate multiple social media. It provides a stream of aggregated posts across multiple social media sources.

At open Summer of code 2017, three students built the Social Media Aggregator for DataScouts. It will get every tweet, status, post or video from different social media channels — and post it all in one place so businesses can stay informed of what is being said online.
